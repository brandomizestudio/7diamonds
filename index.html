Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with 7Diamond Events.

What services does 7Diamond Events offer?

We offer end-to-end wedding management including pre-wedding planning, theme & decor design, event production, guest hospitality, ceremony management (Haldi, Mehendi, Sangeet, Wedding, Reception), photography & videography coordination, and post-wedding services like album coordination and vendor settlements.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for peak wedding season (October-March). For destination weddings, booking 12+ months ahead ensures the best venue availability and vendor coordination.

Do you handle destination weddings?

Yes, we specialize in destination weddings across India and internationally. Our services include venue scouting, luxury guest logistics, curated hospitality experiences, and complete on-ground management at your chosen destination.

What is your budget range?

We work across a wide range of budgets — from elegant mid-range celebrations to ultra-luxury bespoke weddings. During our initial consultation, we'll understand your vision and create a transparent budget plan that maximizes value without compromises.
