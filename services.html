Complete Wedding Management by 7Diamond Events

7Diamond Events is a full-service wedding planning and management company based in Hisar, Haryana, serving clients across India including Delhi NCR, Gurugram, Noida, Chandigarh, Jaipur, and international destinations. We specialize in creating seamless, elegant, and unforgettable wedding experiences that reflect each couple's unique personality and cultural heritage.

Our seven core service departments — Pre-Wedding Planning, Wedding Design & Decor, Event Production, Guest & Hospitality Management, Ceremony Management, Photography & Content, and Post-Wedding Services — work in concert to deliver celebrations that exceed expectations in every detail.

Whether you envision an intimate home gathering or a grand destination celebration, our experienced team brings strategic planning, creative vision, and strong vendor relationships to ensure on-time execution, budget transparency, and a stress-free experience from start to finish.

With over 100 weddings planned and a network of 500+ verified vendors, 7Diamond Events has become a trusted name for wedding management in Haryana and across North India. Let us turn your dream wedding into a beautiful reality.
